Facts about New York City

New York City. The Big Apple. The Concrete Jungle. Its well known that it's America's biggest city and it's financial center but there's plenty more to this city than yellow taxis and the visuals shown in blockbuster movies.

The Abandoned Island

North Brother Island is an abandoned island that sits in the East River, situated between The Bronx and Rikers Island. The island was previously used to quarantine people who had diseases such as small pox and tuberculosis. The island was used for this prupose from 1885 to about 1940.

After World War II, due to a housing shortage, the island was used to house veterans of the war. This remained until the 1950s when the island was used to treat adolescent drug addicts. Since the 1960s the island has been uninhabited and has become somewhat of a bird sanctuary and is off limits to the public.
